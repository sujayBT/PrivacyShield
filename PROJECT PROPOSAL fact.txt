1. TITLE OF THE PROJECT

Factory Management System

2. INTRODUCTION

The Factory Management System (FMS) is a web-based software application designed to automate and manage all factory operations such as employee management, shift scheduling, salary processing, production tracking, inventory control, customer management, and report generation.

Traditional factory management systems depend on manual records, registers, and spreadsheets which leads to data loss, errors, delays, and poor decision making.

This project provides a centralized digital platform that improves productivity, transparency, and efficiency by integrating all factory activities into a single system.

3. OBJECTIVES OF THE PROJECT

• To automate factory operations
• To reduce paperwork and manual errors
• To manage employees and shifts efficiently
• To calculate salary automatically
• To track production shift-wise
• To manage stock inventory
• To handle customer & dealer details
• To generate PDF reports
• To support multi-branch factory management

6. PROBLEM DEFINITION

Factories face problems like poor attendance tracking, salary miscalculation, production delays, inventory issues, and lack of proper reporting.

There is no centralized system to manage shifts, salary, production, and stock efficiently.

The proposed Factory Management System solves these problems by providing a secure, automated, and centralized digital platform.
